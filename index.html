AI in Medicine 2020

Our overall mission is to create a community of multidisciplinary individuals and researchers to help them on the journey of discovery, by proactively educating and connecting them with expertise from interdisplinary backgrounds, with the overall purpose of understanding data to speed deployment of healthcare delivery systems to improve overall care.

Volunteer with us

Nov 18-20 Event Details:

  • The AI in Medicine Datathon focuses on patient health through data from Roswell Park Comprehensive Cancer Center Data Bank & BioRepository. The challenge is to create a model that uses dataset of various cancer cases which includes multiple clinical variables such as cancer stage and morphology to predict patient survival.
  • Build and deploy machine learning / deep learning algorithms and applications.

We envision the datathon to take place during a weekend, to be team-based with up to 5 members per team and collaboration to be facilitated via tools such as Slack, Google Drive, Zoom and Github. The event will include Music Ensemble as part of Music & Medicine Initiative, Graduate Student Presentations, and Industry Leader Discussion Panels. Sponsor prizes will be awarded including the Best Accuracy Model, Best Visual Platform (App or Website), Best Innovative Idea, Best Graduate Presentation as well as People's Choice Awards for each category.
