AI in Medicine Symposium

18-20 NOVEMBER 2020

Our Mission

AI in Medicine's mission is to create a community of multidisplinary individuals and researchers to help them on the journey of discovery, by proactively educating and connecting them with expertise from interdisplinary backgrounds. By understanding the data, we hope to speed deployment of healthcare delivery systems to improve overall care.

How does this 'symposium' work?

COVID-19 pandemic is impacting the care of cancer patients. The AI in Medicine Datathon focuses on cancer patients health through data from Roswell Park Comprehensive Cancer Center Data Bank & BioRepository. The challenge is to create a model that uses dataset of various cancer cases which includes multiple clinical variables such as cancer stage and morphology to predict patient survival.

We envision the datathon to take place during a weekend, to be team-based with up to 5 members per team and collaboration to be facilitated via tools such as Slack, Google Drive, Zoom and Github. The event will include Music Ensemble as part of Music & Medicine Initiative, Graduate Student Presentations, and Industry Leader Discussion Panels.

Prizes will be awarded to the following categories:

  • Best Accuracy & Visual Model
  • Best Innovative Idea
  • Best Graduate Presentation
